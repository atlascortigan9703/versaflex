Our Services

ISO Conversion Tool

ISO 20022 messages are typically formatted in XML, but some legacy systems or protocols might use different formats. Conversion tools can help translate ISO 20022 messages into other formats, such as SWIFT MT (Message Type) messages or proprietary message formats vice versa.

Reconciliation Tool

A Bank Inward and Outward Transactions Reconciliation Tool is a software application used by banks to reconcile and manage financial transactions between different branches of the same bank. The primary purpose of this tool is to ensure accuracy and consistency in the financial records of the bank by reconciling incoming and outgoing transactions among its various branches.
